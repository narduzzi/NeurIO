NeurIO is an end-to-end open source platform for benchmarking embedded machine learning. It's a comprehensive and flexible tool for deploying neural networks on embedded devices that lets engineers and researchers push the state-of-the-art in low power ML, and build and deploy ML-powered applications for IoT.
